# Frequently Asked Questions

## 1. Is the NovaTech Summer Internship Program 2026 paid?

Yes. The internship is paid. Stipend details are shared in the offer letter.

## 2. What is the internship duration?

The internship runs from **June 1, 2026 to August 7, 2026**.

## 3. Can students graduating in 2027 apply?

Yes. Students graduating in **2026, 2027, or 2028** are eligible.

## 4. Can first-year students apply?

First-year students may apply only if their graduation year is 2028 and they meet the eligibility and skill requirements.

## 5. Can I apply to multiple tracks?

Applicants may select one primary track and one backup track.

Final evaluation is based mainly on the primary track.

## 6. Is a GitHub profile mandatory?

A GitHub profile is strongly recommended for technical tracks but not mandatory if the applicant provides another valid project link.

## 7. Is a portfolio mandatory for product design?

Yes. Product Design applicants must submit a portfolio or case study link.

## 8. Can I update my resume after submitting?

Resume correction is allowed only until **March 18, 2026 at 11:59 PM IST** and only for upload errors or corrupted files.

## 9. What file format is required for the resume?

The resume must be uploaded as a **PDF file**.

## 10. What happens if I miss the application deadline?

Late applications are not accepted unless there is a confirmed platform-wide outage.

## 11. Will there be an online assessment?

Most technical tracks have an online assessment from **March 22 to March 24, 2026**.

## 12. How many interviews are there?

Most applicants attend one interview. Some may attend a second interview based on track, score, or review needs.

## 13. Can I use AI tools during the application?

Applicants may use AI tools for drafting and editing, but all claims, projects, and answers must be truthful and personally understood.

## 14. Can I submit a tutorial project?

A tutorial-inspired project is allowed only if the applicant made meaningful original changes and can explain the implementation.

A copied tutorial project with no original contribution is not acceptable.

## 15. Who should I contact for support?

For application support, email `internships-support@novatech.example`.

For assessment issues, email `assessment-help@novatech.example`.

For urgent interview issues, email `interview-coordination@novatech.example`.
