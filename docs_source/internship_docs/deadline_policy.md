# Deadline Policy

## Application Timeline

The NovaTech Summer Internship Program 2026 follows this timeline:

| Event | Date |
|---|---|
| Applications open | February 1, 2026 |
| Application deadline | March 15, 2026 at 11:59 PM IST |
| Resume correction window closes | March 18, 2026 at 11:59 PM IST |
| Online assessment window | March 22–24, 2026 |
| Interview shortlist announcement | April 1, 2026 |
| Interviews | April 5–18, 2026 |
| Final results | April 25, 2026 |
| Offer acceptance deadline | 5 calendar days after offer email |
| Internship start date | June 1, 2026 |
| Internship end date | August 7, 2026 |

## Application Deadline

Applications must be submitted by **March 15, 2026 at 11:59 PM IST**.

Applications submitted after this time are considered late.

## Late Application Policy

Late applications are not accepted unless there is a documented platform outage affecting multiple applicants.

Individual internet issues, laptop problems, exam schedules, or missed reminders do not qualify for deadline extension.

## Resume Correction Window

Applicants who submitted before the deadline may correct resume upload issues until **March 18, 2026 at 11:59 PM IST**.

The correction window may be used only for:

- Replacing a corrupted PDF
- Fixing an accidentally uploaded wrong file
- Uploading a readable version of the same resume

The correction window may not be used to submit a new application after missing the original deadline.

## Assessment Deadline

The online assessment must be completed within the assigned window.

The assessment window is **March 22, 2026 to March 24, 2026**.

No extra attempt is allowed unless the assessment platform confirms a technical failure.

## Interview Scheduling

Applicants must select an interview slot within **48 hours** of receiving the scheduling email.

If an applicant does not select a slot within 48 hours, the recruiting team may assign a slot or move the applicant to the waitlist.

## Rescheduling Policy

Applicants may request one interview reschedule.

Valid reasons include:

- Medical emergency
- University examination
- Family emergency
- Documented connectivity issue

Reschedule requests must be submitted at least **24 hours before** the scheduled interview unless there is an emergency.

## Offer Acceptance Deadline

Selected applicants must accept the offer within **5 calendar days**.

If the offer is not accepted in time, NovaTech may withdraw the offer and contact a waitlisted candidate.
