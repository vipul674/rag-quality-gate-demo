# Remote Work Policy

**Company:** Apex Nimbus Labs  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026  
**Policy Owner:** People Operations Team

## 1. Purpose

This policy explains expectations for employees working remotely or in hybrid arrangements.

## 2. Eligibility

Remote work eligibility depends on role requirements, performance, security needs, and manager approval.

## 3. Working Hours

Employees should maintain core collaboration hours from **10:00 AM to 4:00 PM local time**, unless otherwise approved.

## 4. Availability

Employees are expected to:

- Keep calendars updated.
- Respond to urgent messages within a reasonable timeframe.
- Attend scheduled meetings on time.
- Notify their manager when unavailable during working hours.

## 5. Work Environment

Employees should maintain a workspace that supports productivity, privacy, and safe handling of company information.

## 6. Security Requirements

Remote employees must:

- Use company-approved devices where required.
- Enable device encryption.
- Use multi-factor authentication.
- Avoid public Wi-Fi unless using an approved VPN.
- Never leave confidential material visible in public spaces.

## 7. Performance

Remote work does not change performance expectations. Managers will evaluate employees based on outcomes, collaboration, quality, and reliability.

## 8. Policy Review

Remote work arrangements may be reviewed quarterly or when business needs change.
