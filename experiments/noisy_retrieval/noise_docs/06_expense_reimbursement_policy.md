# Expense Reimbursement Policy

**Company:** Apex Nimbus Labs  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026  
**Policy Owner:** Finance Team

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines how employees may request reimbursement for approved business expenses.

## 2. General Rules

Expenses must be:

- Business-related.
- Reasonable and necessary.
- Supported by receipts.
- Submitted within **30 days** of the expense date.
- Approved by the employee's manager.

## 3. Reimbursable Expenses

Examples include:

- Business travel.
- Approved software subscriptions.
- Client meeting costs.
- Training approved in advance.
- Office supplies for remote work, within budget.

## 4. Non-Reimbursable Expenses

Examples include:

- Personal entertainment.
- Fines or penalties.
- Unapproved upgrades.
- Luxury items.
- Expenses without receipts, unless an exception is approved.

## 5. Travel

Employees should choose cost-effective travel options and follow any booking guidance from Finance.

## 6. Meals

Business meals must include the business purpose and names of attendees.

## 7. Approval

Managers are responsible for reviewing expenses for accuracy, necessity, and policy compliance.

## 8. Fraud

False or misleading reimbursement claims may result in disciplinary action.
