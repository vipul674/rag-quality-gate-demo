# Data Security Policy

**Company:** Apex Nimbus Labs  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026  
**Policy Owner:** Information Security Team

## 1. Purpose

This policy protects company, employee, customer, and partner data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or loss.

## 2. Data Classification

Apex Nimbus Labs uses four data classes:

1. **Public** — Approved for public release.
2. **Internal** — Intended for company personnel only.
3. **Confidential** — Sensitive business, customer, or employee information.
4. **Restricted** — Highly sensitive regulated or security-critical information.

## 3. Access Control

Access is granted based on business need and least privilege.

Employees must:

- Use unique accounts.
- Never share passwords.
- Enable multi-factor authentication.
- Request access through approved channels.
- Remove access when no longer needed.

## 4. Data Storage

Confidential and restricted data must be stored only in approved systems.

## 5. Data Sharing

Before sharing data externally, employees must confirm:

- The recipient is authorized.
- A valid business purpose exists.
- Required agreements are in place.
- The transfer method is approved.

## 6. Incident Reporting

Suspected data loss, phishing, malware, unauthorized access, or accidental exposure must be reported immediately.

## 7. Retention

Data should be retained only as long as needed for business, legal, or compliance purposes.

## 8. Enforcement

Violations may result in access removal, disciplinary action, or legal escalation.
