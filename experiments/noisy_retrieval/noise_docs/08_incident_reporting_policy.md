# Incident Reporting Policy

**Company:** Apex Nimbus Labs  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026  
**Policy Owner:** Risk and Security Team

## 1. Purpose

This policy explains how employees should report workplace, security, compliance, or operational incidents.

## 2. What to Report

Employees should report:

- Data exposure.
- Phishing attempts.
- Lost or stolen devices.
- Workplace safety concerns.
- Harassment or discrimination.
- Policy violations.
- Customer-impacting service issues.
- Fraud, bribery, or unethical conduct.

## 3. Reporting Channels

Incidents may be reported to:

- Direct manager.
- People Operations.
- Security team.
- Legal team.
- Anonymous ethics channel.

## 4. Timing

Security and safety incidents should be reported immediately. Other incidents should be reported as soon as practical.

## 5. Information to Include

Reports should include:

- Date and time.
- People or systems involved.
- Description of what happened.
- Evidence or screenshots, if safe to provide.
- Immediate actions already taken.

## 6. Investigation

Apex Nimbus Labs may investigate reports and take corrective action where needed.

## 7. Confidentiality

Reports will be handled with discretion, subject to investigation and legal requirements.

## 8. Non-Retaliation

Retaliation against a person who reports a concern in good faith is prohibited.
