# Company Code of Conduct

**Company:** Apex Nimbus Labs  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026  
**Policy Owner:** People Operations Team

## 1. Purpose

This policy defines expected professional behavior at Apex Nimbus Labs. The company aims to maintain a respectful, inclusive, ethical, and productive workplace.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, interns, advisors, and temporary workers.

## 3. Core Principles

Employees are expected to:

- Act honestly and ethically.
- Treat coworkers, customers, vendors, and partners with respect.
- Avoid harassment, bullying, discrimination, intimidation, or retaliation.
- Communicate clearly and professionally.
- Protect confidential company and customer information.
- Follow applicable laws, regulations, and internal policies.

## 4. Workplace Respect

Apex Nimbus Labs does not tolerate behavior that creates a hostile, unsafe, or exclusionary work environment. This includes offensive jokes, threats, personal attacks, repeated disruption, or unwanted conduct.

## 5. Conflicts of Interest

Employees must disclose any personal, financial, or outside business interest that could influence their work decisions.

Examples include:

- Working for a competitor.
- Accepting gifts from vendors above reasonable value.
- Hiring or managing a close relative without disclosure.
- Using company information for personal gain.

## 6. Reporting Concerns

Employees may report concerns to their manager, People Operations, Legal, or the anonymous ethics channel.

## 7. Non-Retaliation

Apex Nimbus Labs prohibits retaliation against anyone who reports a concern in good faith.

## 8. Violations

Violations may result in coaching, written warning, suspension, termination, or legal action depending on severity.
