# Leave and Attendance Policy

**Company:** Apex Nimbus Labs  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026  
**Policy Owner:** People Operations Team

## 1. Purpose

This policy explains attendance expectations and leave procedures.

## 2. Attendance Expectations

Employees are expected to:

- Be available during agreed working hours.
- Attend required meetings.
- Notify their manager of delays or absences.
- Record leave accurately in the HR system.

## 3. Types of Leave

Apex Nimbus Labs offers fictional leave categories for sample purposes:

- Annual leave.
- Sick leave.
- Caregiver leave.
- Public holidays.
- Bereavement leave.
- Unpaid leave with approval.
- Learning and development leave.

## 4. Leave Requests

Employees should submit planned leave at least **10 business days** in advance when possible.

## 5. Sick Leave

Employees should notify their manager as soon as reasonably possible. Medical documentation may be requested for extended absences where legally permitted.

## 6. Unplanned Absence

Repeated unplanned absence without reasonable explanation may trigger a manager review.

## 7. Abuse of Leave

Misuse of leave benefits may result in corrective action.
